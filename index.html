Conjure Spooky Queen

Conjure Spooky Queen is a mysterious and powerful leader who emerges in the world of Clash of Clans when night falls. With a haunting appearance and enchanting magical powers, Spooky Queen leads the dark forces to protect your village from the terrifying threats of the night.
